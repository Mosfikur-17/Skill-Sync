How It Works

Understand how Skill Sync guides your career journey using AI, assessments, and data-driven job matching.

Step 1: Register and Create Your Profile

Sign up and enter your skills, interests, and career goals to allow our system to build your personalized career pathway profile.

Step 2: Skill Assessment

Complete AI-powered assessments to identify your current skills and areas for improvement, aligning your competencies with market demand.

Step 3: Receive AI Career Recommendations

Get career path recommendations customized to your interests and skill level, based on real-time labor market insights.

Step 4: Job Matching

Our system matches your profile with available job opportunities, internships, or projects that suit your skillset and preferences.

Step 5: Track Progress and Upskill

Follow your progress, receive targeted course recommendations, and continue to enhance your skills to increase employability and career growth.
