Frequently Asked Questions

What is Skill Sync?

Skill Sync is an AI-powered career pathway advisor that helps users find job opportunities and courses based on their skills and interests.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form.

Is there a fee to use Skill Sync?

No, Skill Sync is completely free to use for all users.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team by clicking the "Contact Us" link in the footer of the website.
